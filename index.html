AI is too complicated for my team. Can we actually do this in-house? +

That's exactly why we exist. We don't hand you a tool and walk away — we train your team hands-on, build the workflows around the way you already work, and leave you with playbooks your staff can follow without any technical background. If your team can use email, they can run these systems.

I already tried ChatGPT and it didn't work for my business. +

ChatGPT is a tool. We give you the workflow, the prompting architecture, and the business-specific context wrapped around it. Most people who "tried AI and it didn't work" were using a general-purpose tool for a specific-purpose problem without the right setup. That's what we fix.

Is $1,000–$2,500 worth it for a small business? +

If this saves your team 10 hours a month at $50/hour — that's $500/month recouped, or $6,000/year. The Quick Launch pays for itself in 2 months. The Full Mastery pays for itself in 5. When an agency builds it, you rent it. When something breaks at 2am, you wait for their support ticket. When their prices go up, you pay more or start over. We build it with you so your team owns it, understands it, and can fix it. No dependency. No monthly management fee. No vendor lock-in.

You fill out a 5-minute audit form. Within 24 hours, Nainoa personally reviews your answers and identifies 2–3 specific AI opportunities for your business. After the session, you get a written proposal with exact scope, timeline, and price — reviewed and approved before anything is charged. No pressure to proceed.

Yes — and we understand Hawaii's market isn't the mainland. Tight local markets, relationship-based sales, smaller staff, and unique industry mix (hospitality, construction, retail, real estate) all factor into how we build your systems. We work with both Hawaii and mainland businesses and adapt accordingly.
