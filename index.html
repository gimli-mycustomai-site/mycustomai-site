FAQ

Common questions

What kind of businesses do you work with?
We work with small to mid-sized businesses across any industry — restaurants, contractors, healthcare offices, real estate agents, retail, e-commerce, service businesses, and more. If you run a business and want to save time with AI, we can build the system.
Do I need any tech experience?
None at all. Our setup guides are written step-by-step — we tell you exactly which platform to use, what to click, and what to type. If you can use a smartphone, you can follow our guides.
How long does the buildout take?
Starter builds are delivered in 48 hours. Pro builds in 72 hours. Heavy buildouts take 5–7 business days. The clock starts once you send us your account credentials.
What accounts do I need to create?
After you fill out the intake form, we'll send you a simple checklist of exactly which tools to sign up for (most are free or low-cost). You create them, send us the login info, and we build everything out.
Is my login info safe?
Yes. We only use your credentials to configure the automations, never to store data or access anything outside the scope of your buildout. You can change passwords after we're done.
Are the tools you recommend expensive?
We only recommend tools with free tiers or affordable pricing for small businesses. Most tools we recommend cost $0–$50/month and pay for themselves quickly in time saved.
Is this really customized for my business?
Yes — your playbook is generated based on your specific business type, size, challenges, and goals. A restaurant gets completely different recommendations than a law firm. Everything is specific to you.
