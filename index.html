FAQ

Common questions

What kind of businesses do you work with?
We work with any small to mid-sized business in Hawaii — restaurants, contractors, salons, retail shops, healthcare offices, real estate agents, accountants, and more. If you have a business and want to save time with AI, we can help.
Do I need any tech experience?
None at all. Our setup guides are written step-by-step — we tell you exactly which website to go to, what to click, and what to type. If you can use a smartphone, you can follow our guides.
How long does it take to get my report?
After you submit your business details and complete payment, your custom PDF report is delivered to your email within minutes.
What if I have more questions after I get my report?
Starter plan customers can upgrade to Pro at any time. Pro plan customers get 30 days of follow-up support included.
Are the tools you recommend expensive?
We only recommend tools that have free tiers or affordable pricing for small businesses. Most of the tools we recommend cost $0–$50/month and pay for themselves quickly in time saved.
How does the consulting session work?
After purchasing the Pro plan, message us directly on WhatsApp to schedule your 1-hour session. We'll meet via video call or phone and walk through your report, answer your questions, and make sure you understand exactly how AI fits into your business and what to do next.
